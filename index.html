About

CHARLENE

Dynamic corporate speaker and consultant introduces a growth mindset practice that helps to view change and the emotions surrounding it as a gift.

As an immigrant to Canada since 2002, Charlene has faced many life challenges. The move has been life changing, with strained family dynamics and a lot of invaluable life lessons. These have taught her how cathartic and affirming starting over can be. Over the past fifteen years, Charlene dared herself and has succeeded at doing many things. Eight years ago she dared herself and garnered a position as a Professor of Human Relations, Sociology, and Living and Working in a Diverse Society at a highly recognized college in Ontario, Canada. Today she has gained the respect from many of her students as an excellent and engaging professor. Another one of her risks was as the owner of a bridal boutique which she established in 2008. This retail establishment lasted five years as Charlene closed in order to focus on her career as a mother, academician and speaker.
Her life experience, coupled with her ability to dare herself in the midst of discord and displacement, and succeed, gives Charlene a unique platform from which to dare others to do the same. Now, as an Empowerment Speaker and Workshop/Funshop facilitator, Charlene challenges others to do what she has done and continues to do: Charlene DARED herself to do what feeds her spirit and is currently on the path to realizing her dreams. In 2013, after an unforeseen life circumstance, she returned to Jamaica and during that time was offered a teaching contract at the University of Technology in Kingston. There she lectured in Entrepreneurship. She returned at the end of the contract and is now continuing her journey in Canada. ​
